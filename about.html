About Us

Virtual Interaction Made Immersive

ZEGOCLOUD revolutionizes virtual engagement with real-time interaction services for hassle-free cloud communication, connecting enterprises, teams, customers and users.

Our journey in audio and video hardware and software development started back in 2001. The core team focused on key technologies in digital signals, algorithms for voice and video, as well as network transmission.

We’ve learned from years of experience that delivering real-time audio and video is no easy feat. It can only be achieved with a wealth of knowledge in many different fields. Yet in this digital era, there are so many use cases for voice and video interaction to build bridges and forge links.

So we started to explore how to share our passion for and expertise in audio and video solutions with more businesses and developers as a partner in their journey of success. That’s why we founded ZEGOCLOUD in 2015 .

With Real-Time Communication (RTC) empowered by AI, ZEGOCLOUD now seeks to turn the vision for Real-Time Interaction (RTI) into a reality. We lay the groundwork for businesses and developers to build RTI into their apps across mobile internet and the metaverse. Flexible, customizable, low code.

What we build

2015

ZEGOCLOUD RTC voice engine was launched, and real-time interaction service can be accessed with just 4 lines of code.

2016

ZEGOCLOUD RTC audio and video engine was released. It supports audio and video live streaming, pioneered the live streaming co-hosting interactive technology, and has launched long-term cooperation with many leading clients.

2017

A scalable multi-cloud global network for real-time audio, video, and data transmission deployed. Smooth real-time communication even at 70% packet loss.It supports 32-way simultaneous interaction.

2018

ZEGOCLOUD RTC engine (Gen. 2) launched. Compatible with mainstream signaling and transmission protocols, audio and video codecs, as well as various languages and frameworks,covering more than 15,000 devices.

2019

The 5G audio and video laboratory was inaugurated and launched, supporting 4K high-definition transmission. Analytics Dashboard launched. Making it possible to monitor call quality and troubleshoot based on intelligent analytics.

2020

The average daily real-time interaction duration has exceeded 3 billion minutes. In-app Chat, Cloud Recording, SuperBoard, and AI Effects launched. Further expanding our RTI product line to meet the rising demand for remote collaboration and online learning during the pandemic.

2021

Prebuilt low-code products like UIKits and one-stop solutions for social entertainment and education launched. Allowing businesses and developers to easily build infrastructure for remote work.

2022

Upgraded to RTI platform from RTC engine, it supports ten thousand people to turn on their microphones at the same time. The privatized audio and video middle platform has been launched to comprehensively empower the financial industry.

2023

Released a series of AI digital human products. Joined hands with dozens of ecological partners to launch the ZEGOCLOUD Cloud Marketplace, aiming to meet the diverse needs of customers.

2024

Fully implemented the RTC + AI strategy, launched AI Agent interactive solutions, and launched the Purio AI audio engine.

2025

Our journey continues…
